# ✅ Fix (do this exactly)

## 🔓 Step 1 — Open Admin PowerShell

* Press **Start**
* Type: `powershell`
* Right-click → **Run as Administrator**
* Click **Yes**

You should see:

```id="d7y2kq"
Administrator: Windows PowerShell
```

---

## 🔧 Step 2 — Run the commands again

Now paste this:

```powershell id="8f9x2c"
dism /online /disable-feature /featurename:Microsoft-Windows-Subsystem-Linux /norestart
dism /online /disable-feature /featurename:VirtualMachinePlatform /norestart
dism /online /disable-feature /featurename:Hyper-V-All /norestart
```

---

## 🔁 Step 3 — Restart your PC

**Important:** Click **Restart** (not shutdown)

---

## 🔧 Step 4 — Re-enable everything

After reboot, again open **Admin PowerShell** and run:

```powershell id="v4j2lo"
dism /online /enable-feature /featurename:Microsoft-Windows-Subsystem-Linux /all /norestart
dism /online /enable-feature /featurename:VirtualMachinePlatform /all /norestart
dism /online /enable-feature /featurename:Hyper-V-All /all /norestart
```
